Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 4, 2026

PDF Reader Pro ("the App") is developed by balhaddad-sys. This policy describes how the App handles your data.

Data We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All PDF files you open are processed entirely on your device.

AI Assistant Feature

The App includes an optional AI assistant powered by Google Gemini. When you use this feature:

  • The text content (or images) of the currently viewed PDF pages are sent to the Google Gemini API to generate answers to your questions.
  • Your questions and the AI responses are stored locally on your device only and are not sent to any other server.
  • No personal identifiers are included in API requests.

Google's use of data sent to the Gemini API is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

All app data (annotations, bookmarks, settings) is stored locally on your device using IndexedDB. Nothing is uploaded to external servers.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Gemini API — used only when you actively use the AI assistant feature.

No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are included in the App.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please open an issue at github.com/balhaddad-sys/pdf-reader-pro.

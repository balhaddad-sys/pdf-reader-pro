Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 16, 2026

PDF Reader Pro ("the App") is developed by balhaddad-sys. This policy describes how the App handles your data.

Data We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All PDF files you open are processed and stored entirely on your device using IndexedDB.

AI Assistant Feature

The App includes an optional AI assistant powered by Anthropic's Claude API. When you use this feature:

  • The text content (or screenshot images) of the currently viewed PDF pages are sent to the Anthropic Claude API via our server-side proxy to generate answers to your questions.
  • Only the pages near your current view are sent — not the entire document.
  • Your questions and the AI responses are stored locally on your device only.
  • No personal identifiers are included in API requests.
  • Chat history is scoped per document and is cleared when you switch documents.

Anthropic's use of data sent to the Claude API is governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

If you do not use the AI assistant feature, no data leaves your device.

Data Storage

All app data (documents, annotations, bookmarks, settings) is stored locally on your device using IndexedDB. Nothing is uploaded to external servers except as described in the AI Assistant section above.

Third-Party Services

  • Anthropic Claude API — used only when you actively use the AI assistant feature.
  • Google Fonts — the Inter font is loaded from Google's CDN for typography.

No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are included in the App.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please open an issue at github.com/balhaddad-sys/pdf-reader-pro.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

PDF Reader Pro is a fully offline, privacy-first app. Your documents stay on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data or document content.

1. Information We Collect

We collect no personal information. PDF Reader Pro operates entirely on your device. No accounts, no sign-ups, no tracking.

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • PDF files you open (stored in your device's IndexedDB for offline access)
  • Reading progress, bookmarks, and annotations you create
  • Your app preferences (theme, scroll mode, zoom)

None of this data ever leaves your device.

2. Internet Usage

The app requests internet permission for the following purposes only:

  • Google Fonts — to load display fonts (Source Serif 4, DM Sans). These requests contain no personal data. If you are offline, the app falls back to system fonts.

PDF files you open are never sent over the internet. All PDF processing happens on-device.

3. Storage

PDF Reader Pro stores data locally using:

  • IndexedDB — for storing PDF file data on your device
  • localStorage — for your preferences, bookmarks, and annotations

You can delete all stored data at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings, or by using the delete function within the app.

4. Permissions

PDF Reader Pro requests the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET — required only to load display fonts from Google Fonts
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / READ_MEDIA_DOCUMENTS — to open PDF files you select from your device storage

We do not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other sensitive data.

5. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) — for typography. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • pdf.js (Mozilla) — for PDF rendering, bundled locally. No network requests made by pdf.js.

6. Children's Privacy

PDF Reader Pro does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains no advertising, in-app purchases, or social features.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Google Play Store listing.

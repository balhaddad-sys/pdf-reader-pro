Place your app icon here as:
  resources/icon.png         (1024×1024 PNG, no transparency — required)
  resources/splash.png       (2732×2732 PNG — optional, for splash screen)
  resources/icon-background.png  (1024×1024, solid color for adaptive icon — optional)

Then run:
  npx capacitor-assets generate

This generates all required Android launcher icon sizes:
  android/app/src/main/res/mipmap-mdpi/ic_launcher.png      (48×48)
  android/app/src/main/res/mipmap-hdpi/ic_launcher.png      (72×72)
  android/app/src/main/res/mipmap-xhdpi/ic_launcher.png     (96×96)
  android/app/src/main/res/mipmap-xxhdpi/ic_launcher.png    (144×144)
  android/app/src/main/res/mipmap-xxxhdpi/ic_launcher.png   (192×192)
  Plus adaptive icon foreground/background layers for Android 8+

TIPS:
- Use a 1024×1024 PNG exported from your design tool (Figma, Photoshop, etc.)
- Background should be solid (no transparency) for the Play Store listing
- Keep important content within the inner 75% of the canvas (safe zone for adaptive icons)
- The Play Store also needs a 512×512 PNG for the store listing icon
